Our Eternal Guide

Pujya Maharaj Ji

"Shri 1008 Shri Jagannathdas Ji Maharaj"

A Life of Absolute Renunciation

The spiritual architecture of the Shri Rishabh Tirth Trust stands firmly upon the intense penance, pure vision, and unwavering devotion of Pujya Maharaj Ji. His earthly timeline reflects a continuous journey away from worldly illusions toward eternal cosmic alignment.

Early Life

Auspicious Birth

Born into a pious family in **Uttar Pradesh**, India. From early childhood, Maharaj Ji displayed an inherent detachment toward transient worldly pleasures, spending prolonged hours in deep, quiet contemplative states that signaled his higher spiritual calling.

Ayodhya Dham

Sacred Diksha

Driven by an intense internal thirst for absolute truth, he renounced his home and journeyed to the sacred land of Ayodhya Dham. There, he found refuge at the lotus feet of the revered Shri Shri 1008 Shri Ramdas Ji Maharaj (popularly known as Dada Gurudev, Near Badi Chawani). Under his divine guidance, he received holy initiation and was bestowed with the spiritual title Shri Jagannathdas Ji Maharaj.

Sitamani Caves

Mauna Vrat

Seeking profound, uninterrupted communion with the divine, Maharaj Ji entered the deep, isolated Sitamani Caves. Here, he undertook an extraordinary "Mauna Vrat" (vow of absolute silence) for years, channeling his mental energies inward and mastering advanced levels of yogic consciousness. According to sacred tradition, these caves are believed to have sheltered Bhagwan Shri Ram Ji, Mata Sita Ji, and Lakshman Ji during their exile in a bygone age, further enhancing the spiritual significance of this revered place.

Trust Epicenters

Establishments

Emerging from intense penance filled with divine grace, Maharaj Ji traveled extensively across various states to anchor hubs of spiritual recovery. He systematically established the four main branches: **Damaudhara (Sakti), Korba, Rishikesh, and Ujjain**, establishing permanent sanctuaries for spiritual seekers.

Dec 15, 2008

Mahaprayan

Having perfectly completed his earthly mission and spiritual manifestations, Maharaj Ji merged his physical form back into the cosmic elements through **Holy Mahaprayan** on December 15, 2008, at the primary branch of Rishabh Tirth. His divine energy continues to guide the trust's path.

The Teachings

Core Ideals Imparted

तप

Tapasya (Austerity)

Maharaj Ji strongly emphasized that true inner clarity is forged through silence, self-discipline, and sustained contemplative reflection, away from sensory noise.

सेवा

Sewa (Selfless Service)

"Feeding a hungry soul is equivalent to serving the Supreme Being directly." This profound standard underpins our extensive, ongoing public welfare campaigns.

यज्ञ

Yagya (Sacred Order)

Preserving the meticulous chanting of Vedic mantras to purify environmental energies and uplift humanity’s shared collective consciousness.

Shri Rishabh Tirth Trust

Preserving classical dynamic Vedic paths and managing multi-branch historical ashrams under dedicated lines of ascetic lineage parameters.
