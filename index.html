Pujya Maharaj Ji Biography

Shri Jagannathdas Ji

Tirobhav Tithi — 2008

Auspicious Inception

Birth in Uttar Pradesh & Spiritual Inclination

Born with a profound detachment towards transient material realities, leaving his homeland early in life in quest of absolute truth.

Sacred Initiation

Initiation by Shri Shri 1008 Shri Ramdas Ji Maharaj

Acquired ultimate classical wisdom at Ayodhya Dham. Initiated into supreme ascetic order, receiving the name Shri Jagannathdas Ji Maharaj.

Severe Penance

Mauna Vrat at Sitamani Caves

Withdrew entirely from physical speech elements, executing years of profound internal silence (Mauna Vrat) deep within the historic Sitamani Caves.

Mission Expansion

Spiritual Establishments & Yajna Traditions

Constructed key ashram strongholds over major divine domains while spearheading vast lines of sacred public Vedic Mahavishnu Yajnas.

Divine Integration

Holy Agni Samadhi — 15 December 2008

Transcended the temporary biological sheath at Rishabh Tirth through holy Agni Samadhi, establishing an undying presence in the hearts of thousands.
