Our Mandirs & Ashrams

Explore the four sacred seats of intense penance, continuous Vedic worship, and humanitarian service established by Pujya Maharaj Ji.

Primary Headquarters

Shri Rishabh Tirth Ashram

Damaudhara, Sakti, Chhattisgarh

Deeply rooted within historical epic poetry, this sacred setting is referenced directly in the Mahabharata heritage texts. It serves as the spiritual epicenter of the Trust, offering an unparalleled environment where raw, serene nature merges seamlessly with daily Vedic rituals.

Spiritual & Structural Features:
Ram Darbar & Radha Govind Lakshmi Narayan & Hanuman Ji Rishabhdev Bhagwan Sanctum Maharaj Ji Samadhi Sthal Akhand Dhuni & Yajnashala Active Gaushala (Cow Shelter) Rishabh Tirth Perennial Waterfall

Shri Ram Janaki Mandir

Sitamani, Korba, Chhattisgarh

The historic site where Pujya Maharaj Ji spent years in absolute silence (Mauna Vrat). The cave complex holds a highly charged meditative atmosphere, providing seekers with a profound, quiet environment to dive deep into their internal spiritual tracking.

Spiritual & Structural Features:
Sacred Ancient Ram Gufa Historic Ram Darbar Deities Perpetual Akhand Dhuni Flame Isolated Meditation Zones
Request Cave Access
Himalayan Retreat

Shri Ram Janaki Mandir & Tapovan Ashram

Tapovan, Rishikesh, Uttarakhand

Perched beautifully on the sweeping banks of the holy River Ganga in Rishikesh. This ashram provides passing saints, pilgrims, and lifelong practitioners a quiet Himalayan refuge to deepen their sadhana amidst the pure sound waves of the sacred river.

Spiritual & Structural Features:
Direct Access to River Ganga Banks Traditional Ram Darbar Sanctum Bajrangbali Temple Complex Dedicated Sadhu Lodging & Accommodation
Sadhana Registration

Shri Ram Janaki Mandir

Sodang, Ujjain, Madhya Pradesh

Situated within the vibrant Mahakaleshwar horizon at Sodang, Ujjain. It retains a highly auspicious identity centered around continuous daily services, pristine protection of indigenous cattle breeds, and historic traditions honoring the holy footprints of the divine matrix.

Spiritual & Structural Features:
Mahakaleshwar Holy Region Horizon Ancient Installed Vedic Deities Active Gaushala Cattle Sanctuary Sacred Sita Rasoi Heritage Kitchen
